Dispute Workflow

How score, claim, source, and verdict issues should be challenged.

What can be disputed?

  • A claim was extracted from a tweet incorrectly.
  • A claim has a better source than the one attached to the verdict.
  • A verdict should be changed to CONFIRMED, REFUTED, UNVERIFIED, or EXPIRED.
  • A journalist handle is incorrect, inactive, duplicated, or missing.
  • A score changed because of a data coverage issue rather than real evidence.

Evidence required

  1. Journalist handle and affected claim text.
  2. Original tweet URL or tweet ID when available.
  3. Source URL that supports the requested change.
  4. Short explanation of why the current verdict is wrong or incomplete.

Review policy

SourceRank should favor conservative changes: keep claims unresolved when evidence is weak, prefer tier-one source links for confirmation or refutation, and avoid treating commentary as factual claims. Disputed rows should be rechecked with `python pipeline/verifier.py --recheck` after the news corpus has been refreshed.
